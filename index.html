How does the app work?

Language learning is made very easy for you. It is a simple question-answer model. You get questions based on the many topics you can choose and the questions can be answered in the most creative way possible. The AI will highlight if there are any mistakes and you have the chance to correct your answers right away.

Why learn German with Deutschgenie?

Learning made fun - A great variety of cool visual cues to support the learning experience. Deutschgenie uses great, proven methods by top level teachers. The combination of color and icon feedback helps the learner recognize patterns faster and get adapted to german grammar much more quickly.
